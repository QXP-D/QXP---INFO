# Privacy Policy for QXP Network Application

**Effective Date:** June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the QXP Application ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects, processes, stores, and protects data gathered from Discord users and servers ("Guilds"). By initializing your profile, registering a Network ID, or interacting with the application, you explicitly agree to the automated data processing behaviors detailed in this document.

## 1. Explicit Data We Collect & Store
To maintain a functional, persistent, and multi-tenant text-based roleplay economy, our secure database clusters log the following operational categories:
*   **User Identifiers:** Discord User Snowflake IDs (`userId`), Discord Usernames, and custom unique text handles voluntarily provided during system initialization (`networkId`).
*   **Guild Configurations:** Discord Server IDs (`guildId`), text channel markers for world event notifications (`eventChannelId`), and customized starting wallet limits.
*   **Economic & Game States:** Mathematical records of virtual progression, including liquid cash balances, upgraded bank tiers (`bankLevel`), outstanding loan structures, real-time compounding credit variables, corporate business array structures, crypto portfolio holdings, and inventory mapping arrays.
*   **AI Sentinel Security Logs:** Automated tracking logs containing timestamps, evaluated risk ratings (`LOW`, `MEDIUM`, `HIGH`, `CRITICAL`), command failure diagnostics, and executive disciplinary transaction history logs.
*   **Analytics Matrix:** A quiet background metric tracking total command execution volume (`commandsUsed`) and system entry timestamps (`joinedAt`) for analytics display.

## 2. What We Explicitly Do NOT Collect
*   We **never** read, log, cache, monitor, or store human conversational text messages within your channels or direct messages.
*   We **never** request, process, or harvest real-world personal data, including real names, email addresses, private passwords, physical location telemetry, or payment credentials.
*   The Bot works strictly through active, user-initiated Slash Command contexts.

## 3. Core Mechanics Data Utilization
All indexed variables are used strictly to maintain in-game features within your designated Discord server environments:
*   Persisting item inventories, business level tracking, and cryptocurrency allocations.
*   Enforcing programmatic game constraints, including command cooldown periods, compounding interest tracking, and bankruptcy freezes.
*   Routing randomized world financial news alerts to text channels specified by server administrators.

## 4. Data Retention, Purges & Erasure Rights
*   **Retention:** Data profiles are stored persistently within our MongoDB Atlas environment to ensure your progression is saved safely between active gameplay sessions.
*   **Automated Purges:** If the Bot is kicked or removed from a Guild, server-specific configuration files are retained but remain inert. 
*   **Operative Erasure Rights:** Any user can request the permanent, absolute deletion of their entire financial and structural profile from our database array. Deletion requests can be initiated via an profile reset command or by contacting our administrative team through the official support channel. All requests are permanently processed and cleared within 72 hours.

## 5. Third-Party Data Disclosures
We maintain a strict stance on data isolation. We do not sell, trade, rent, lease, or distribute user data, analytics, or transaction logs to third-party ad networks, data brokers, or external monetization engines. Data is safely stored and processed within our isolated hosting pipelines (Render) and database backends (MongoDB Atlas).

## 6. Regulatory Contact & Support
For data privacy inquiries, access audit requests, or technical deletion questions, please contact the development staff directly via the official QXP support server network link displayed on the application profile card.
